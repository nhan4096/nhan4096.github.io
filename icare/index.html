Interface Containment and Research Effort

The Interface Containment and Research Effort (shortened as ICaRE), is a specialized division made by the Brainrot Group. The central entity being studied is an anomalous channel named Ultra Interface, thereafter referred to as UI.


UI is part of a larger alliance known as Xitray Team, abbreviated as XT. Another person of interest also from XT is T6, the details of which are also partly researched within ICaRE.


ICaRE's goal is to contain and research UI, NT (known as T6 in alternate Brainrot documentation, see Project T6. Note that he uses a different persona to that of T6 or NT online.) and other Xitray Team-associated anomalies.
