Fragment 007

Motion 9016.9016: Speech is both immoral and a significant limitation towards humanity.

A voting scheme has been considered unneeded as this statement is an obvious axiom in the immoral creation of humanity.

Result: Motion 9016.9016 passed with approximately 8.2 billion unanimous "yea" votes.


Fragment 008

iv. Accept defeat to the Order and concede

The solution entails the defeat and concession of the Foundation to the Order of the Silent (GOI-9016).

While it does not resolve SCP-9016, a resolution to this problem has been deemed unneeded and extremely immoral.

For brevity, the advantages of this solution have been omitted. There are no limitations to society if there is no speech; in fact, speech was a major limitation to humanity while it existed.


Item #: 9016

Object Class: Uncontained

Secure Containment Procedures: Containment of SCP-9016 has been deemed unnecessary and immoral. The effects of SCP-9016, while major, do not cause any negative maladies insofar as the containment of it is needed.
SCP-9016-A is to be uncontained.

Description: SCP-9016 is an event too banal to be described. SCP-9016-A is a person too banal to be described.
