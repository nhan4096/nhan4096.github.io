Item #: 9016

Object Class: Declassified

Secure Containment Procedures: Containment of the knowledge of SCP-9016 is deemed unattainable due to the nature of the anomaly and how extensively it affects humanity. Primary containment is instead to focus on reversing the effects of SCP-9016.

Description: SCP-9016 is a LNK-Class Linguistic Collapse scenario, happening shortly following the phrase "Câm mồm!" being uttered by a closely related anomalous entity, further referred to as SCP-9016-A. Sounds produced by biologically produced vibrations which are intentional (e.g. speech, grunting or singing) of all species which have them are, succeeding the manifestation of SCP-9016, completely incapable to be vocalized. Attempts at vocalization only result in failure; as the vibration of the vocal flaps only result in a frequency of ~0.006736Hz, significantly below animal hearing levels.

Addendum 9016.1: SCP-9016 Timeline

The following chart depicts the approximate timeline in which the events related to SCP-9016 occurred.

Time Event
T-2s Decibels measured within the proximity of SCP-9016-A is 90dB.
T-1.3s Begin vocalization of the aforementioned anomalous phrase by SCP-9016-A.
T+0s End vocalization.
T+0.029s Vocal cords within a five-meter range (i.e. fully dispersed throughout the room where SCP-9016-A was present at the time) of SCP-9016-A succumb to the effect. Decibels measured in SCP-9016-A's proximity rapidly drop to 23dB.
T+1.1s Vocal cords worldwide succumb to the effect; considered as SCP-9016's completion.

Addendum 9016.2: Interview with SCP-9016-A

After a series of geolocation attempts, Foundation researchers were able to triangulate SCP-9016-A's coordinates to a singular building in ███████████ ████. The following is a transcript of a digital conversation (due to the circumstances presented by the ongoing SCP-9016 crisis) held between Dr. Mintay and SCP-9016-A.

<begin transcript>

Dr. Mintay: Hello. Please state your designation

SCP-9016-A: SCP-9016-A. I do not think that your christened name will last very long, though.

Dr. Mintay: Let us begin the interview. Why did you say that phrase?

SCP-9016-A: Both of us know. You and the rest of your species come here, on my planet to infiltrate and to trouble me with your cowardly mouths. I loathe and seethe over the sound — sorry, noise of your kind. Order must be given, and it is only given by me, to you.

Dr. Mintay: Interesting... Expand, please?

SCP-9016-A: Not just your kind, but also what your planet surrounds itself with. Those pestlike birds haunt your ears, but why do you not notice? Those bothersome animals.

Dr. Mintay: Why are we and the animals, apparently, bothersome?

SCP-9016-A: Your mouths and whatever else make nothing but noise. Sound, meaningful sound only reveals itself from objects you say are "noise". The fans in plane engines or those in your home, the air conditioning unit that rattles at just the right amount, the fridge humming and buzzing or that light, right there in this room, the light I can hear clearly and I enamor it. You yourself are noise. Then there are the animals. If they had complex thought, and this extends to you, they would've silenced themselves long ago.

Dr. Mintay: Where does your power come from?

SCP-9016-A: It was given to me since I have existed. I was not born, but I seem to have only manifested in a sudden burst. I did not have my own volition then, but I am more than content with how I can make you bestow silence and reticence to me at will now.

SCP-9016-A: I firmly ask you not to try and "reverse" my efforts. I am trying to help you to protect you from yourselves.

SCP-9016-A: Please, I must go.

Dr. Mintay: Thank you for your time.

SCP-9016-A: Benevolence is not needed for theological deities, I believe, doctor.

<end transcript>

When He ordered silence to us, we bestowed silence back to Him. If He had this power, He is to be considered as a true deity, a deity to follow. We do not understand why you consider humanity in this manner. We were meant to be creatures that made, we were not meant to be creatures that only spinelessly, absentmindedly received what your supposed "deity" (known in our documents as SCP-9016-A; we would prefer it if you referred to it in this way) or whoever else says.

As such, the Silencing (known in our documents as SCP-9016; please also refer to this event this way) is an event to be reversed. Speech is essential for humanity, for what we made, for how we talked to each other and, even more selflessly, for how everything communicated. From the birds outside our facilities singing to the least cherished of creatures, cockroaches hissing, they are all equally important. If this "impurity" of humanity was such a selfish act, should the preservation of the muteness of all other animals not be considered as selfish as well?

— SCP Foundation. Secure. Contain. Protect.


Both Him and the Silencing are to be admired. To disrespect Him is to disrespect an entity, who we do not know how much power the entity possesses. To disrespect Him is akin to disrespecting the mother of the planet, the mother of the universe itself. To disrespect the Silencing is to disrespect what He had made. To disrespect the Silencing is to disrespect what He had made, to protect us from ourselves, from our own rot of creation itself.

Let us redefine the rot of creation. The rot that is creation is a selfish act, punishable as a sin. He has power to both help us, seen through the Silencing arriving and to force us to endure endless tribulations, through this rot of creation. If we do not follow him, humanity will be rotten with the act of creation. We do not know why He had to protect us from this rot, but we only know that it is rot. We do not know what will happen to us if we do not listen to Him.

We are not creators. We are recoils and derivatives of Him.

— Order of the Silent


What evidence do you have that creation is something to be left behind, as a form of "rot"? The act of creation is not inherently selfish, unlike what you say. We create not only for the enjoyment of ourselves (which is what we presume to be the “selfish” part of creation, according to you) but also for what others can receive from it. We create for others and not just ourselves, and it is for this reason that creation is not selfish.

If creation were to be selfish, why should inventors like Thomas Edison not be berated for their inventions? Why did they create the inventions? The point of creating a lightbulb is not for Edison's enjoyment, but for the advancement of the knowledge of humanity and for the convenience of the entirety of humanity.

Next, you mention SCP-9016-A in the same light as a deity, but we would like to rectify that this is not true. You allude to humanity being silenced after SCP-9016-A yelled that phrase as evidence for its power, but you can not prove that SCP-9016-A is truly powerful. It is completely possible that the only power of SCP-9016-A is to silence humanity completely and nothing else. You can not conclude that it is an "omnipotent and all knowing deity" since it did this one anomalous act.

Furthermore, you refer to the “tribulations” we will go through if we do not follow the alleged deity. We believe that absentmindedly consuming knowledge as prescribed by SCP-9016-A is actively harmful towards humanity, much more so than genuinely and actively producing content. During the ten thousand years we existed as a civilization, there were no visible tribulations that this supposed deity would make us go through. During the golden age of Greece, of India, of Europe, or the Industrial Revolutions where inventions were being created in rapid succession, were any of us punished for this supposed "sin"?

We think that your rhetoric is actively harmful towards the advancement of humanity, and as an organization primarily focused on the protection of humanity, we can not and will never accept your ideology. Your rhetoric is nothing but suppositions without fact or evidence. To repeat what was said, SCP-9016 and SCP-9016-A are to be reversed and destroyed, and this eon be remembered as a dark time for humanity, not to be cherished nor be nostalgic about.

— SCP Foundation. Secure. Contain. Protect.

Let us address your points.

Addendum 9016.5: Ethics Committee Memo
The following email was sent as a memo from the Ethics Committee to the O5 Council as part of a discussion on the morality of speech mentioned in the meeting.

From: alexmpearson@scip.net
To: o5.council@scip.net

Dear O5 Council,

I would like to start this message by stating that speech is moral. The remaining contents of this email is to give reasoning as to why.

Firstly, speech is what defines us, and it is one of the core attributes that makes us, us. All of our advancements had to be made from some sort of communication, from person to person. The knowledge of this advancement must be known in some regard, which is why oral speech is such an effective manner in this regard. Since the advent of humanity, which I would like to argue is the period where humanity was "purest" (i.e. our nature was most discernible at this stage), we have been implicitly and explicitly communicating with each other; linguistic theories give us reasons to what we could have been vocalizing at the start, but, herein this debate on the morality of speech, we do not need to give an explicit answer to what we were saying initially. Without speech, we would not be ourselves.

Next, there is no manner in which speech can be viewed as immoral which is also logical. Repeating the previous point, speech is a crucial part of us and saying that speech is immoral would be to call ourselves immoral. Note that we can not call ourselves, as a collective, immoral as it is categorically impossible: we can not label a species immoral as, at that point, we are not describing anything meaningful. Any train of thought which considers speech immoral is simply absurd.

Finally, the relevant event not only affects humanity but also, much broader, almost all animals. To say that speech is immoral is also to say that other animals are "immoral" for possessing speech as well. Similar reasoning can be used from the second argument to explain why animals speaking can not be considered immoral.

Moreover, although it is not related to the reasoning of the morality of speech, I would like to suggest that we terminate contact with GOI-9016. It seems that the points that GOI-9016 have been making recently are being repeated and no amount of persuasion by us will change their mind. Continuation of conversation with them seems like a waste of time if they continue to be stubborn like what we have seen so far.

I hope this email finds you well,
Dr. Alex M. Pearson

Addendum 9016.8: Research on the Reversal of SCP-9016

Attached is the paper regarding the research of the reversal of SCP-9016.

I. Identifying the cause of SCP-9016

So far, within this investigation, we have linked SCP-9016-A's incantation as a direct cause to SCP-9016. We still have not answered why SCP-9016-A's power exists. Let us run appropriate tests to identify the type of power that SCP-9016-A possesses.

i. These two variables are not causal but only correlated

We have identified that this is not true as there are no other possible causes of SCP-9016-A other than SCP-9016-A.

ii. SCP-9016 was caused by SCP-9016-A modifying the physiology of vocal cords within a past timeline

Per anomalously modified atomic clocks 1—137 currently running at Site-19, there were no disruptions to reality at any time which affected vocal cords. Note that the atomic clocks have been running since T+0.15s after the Big Bang.

iii. SCP-9016-A is a reality bender

After a Hume test of SCP-9016-A was performed, levels were detected to be significantly lower than baseline reality levels. Therefore, this conclusion has been determined to be true.

iv. SCP-9016-A is a Class O-Ontological reality bender

No ontological shifts were detected at the time of SCP-9016.

v. SCP-9016-A is a Class P-Physical reality bender

Effects of SCP-9016-A were propagated in an outward manner, where the vocal cords of individuals affected had changed physically. Therefore, this conclusion has been determined likely to be true.

vi. SCP-9016-A is a theological deity

An Akiva radiation test was performed on SCP-9016-A; levels were found to be significantly higher than baseline. Therefore, this conclusion has been determined to be true.

vii. Final conclusion

SCP-9016-A is a Class P-Physical reality bender and theological deity. The power of SCP-9016-A was derived from these attributes, thus causing SCP-9016 when the incantation was spoken aloud.

II. Possible solutions

We suggest possible solutions with their respective advantages and limitations. It is of notice due to the nature of the incident, it may recur without prior indication.

i. Manual individual physical reconfiguration of vocal cords

Solution entails the surgery (or other physical method) of reconfiguring vocal cords.

Advantages:

  • Most plausible and possibly requiring the least research out of other solutions;
  • Able to be distributed worldwide easily via the training of surgeons on the surgery.

Limitations:

  • Exorbitantly costly both money-wise and time-wise approach, will have to be distributed to more than eight billion humans and more significantly, numerous affected nonhuman species;
  • Does not prevent the incident from recurring;
  • Does not provide any changes if the incident recurs, must be redone should the SCP-9016 recur;
  • May not work as SCP-9016-A is a theological deity, as such, vocal cords may be affected in a non-physical manner.

ii. Temporal reversal of affected timeline

Solution entails the reversal of the affected timeline and supplanting baseline reality with an unaffected timeline, without the existence of SCP-9016-A and SCP-9016.

Advantages:

  • Prevents the incident from recurring as SCP-9016-A does not exist;
  • The effect is immediately worldwide, hence, the solution is extensive.

Limitations:

  • Research may take too long and consume too much monetarily;
  • Solution may be impossible if SCP-9016-A is omnipresent and thus not able to be eradicated completely; this is plausible as SCP-9016-A has been proven to be a religious deity.

iii. Deicide of SCP-9016-A

Solution entails the deicide of SCP-9016-A.

Advantages:

  • Prevents the incident from recurring as SCP-9016-A does not exist;

Limitations:

  • Solution may be impossible as SCP-9016-A has been proven to be a religious deity, thus it may be omnipresent and immortal;
  • Solution does not ensure that SCP-9016 will be reversed, only ensuring that SCP-9016 will not recur;
  • Does not resolve SCP-9016 immediately, should it be resolved via the solution.
