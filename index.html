Project T6 is a satirical (yes, again!) terminal-based game that is a parody of my classmate, known as T6. It is a text-based game that contains a lot of inside jokes. The sequel to this game is ICaRE. Don't spoil yourself by looking at the code!!!

Anagrammer Game is a game where you have to guess the anagrams of words, i.e. rearrange the letters of a string to form a word. It is far simpler than other games (in inside joke complexity), so it is recommended for newcomers (but still, please give me your data on the Puzzles login page). It is the calm after and before the storm. Think of it as the buffer zone between Project T6 and ICaRE.

ICaRE is a satirical (again...) heavily text-based Alternate Reality Game that is a parody of Ultra Interface and T6 (see Project T6). It is based on SCP-style writing, containing multiple elements of fiction, bureaucracy and a lot of satire. It is also a logical successor to Project T6, however dialed up, wrapped in red tape and worldbuilding. It's a work in progress, so expect more updates. I'd estimate that it has around 13000-ish words in it and an hour of content, at least? The name is a backronym from the phrase "I care", in deliberate opposition to that of the dismissive arseholes known as the Xitray Group (containing Ultra Interface and T6, among others) who say "Who cares?".
